Our Story

Where Sri Lankan Heritage Meets Modern Craft

KōpiKadē (කෝපි කඩේ) - meaning "coffee shop" in Sinhala - is more than a name. It's a promise to honor the rich coffee culture of Sri Lanka while pushing the boundaries of what a modern café can be.

We're building a space where cutting-edge brewing technology, sustainable sourcing, and warm Sri Lankan hospitality come together to create something truly unforgettable.
